NSec Remote Signer User Guide

Getting Started

The NSec Remote Signer is a remote signer for Nostr. It stores your private keys on the device and allows you to use Nostr clients without exposing your private keys to your computer or other devices.

Device Setup

  1. Your device will be shipped with firmware installed. You will need to connect it to your computer and enter setup mode to configure it.
  2. Hold button 1 while connecting to your computer with a USB cable
  3. Use the configuration tool on this website to configure your device
  4. Enter the following information:
    • WiFi SSID and password
    • Nostr private key
    • Relay URL
  5. Click the "Save" button to save the configuration
  6. You should see a message indicating that the device is configured
  7. You can now use your device to sign messages

Usage

Only button 1 is currently supported. Press and hold it to display a pairing QR code. Scan the QR code with your nostr client to pair with the device.
